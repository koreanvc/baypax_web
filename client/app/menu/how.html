How It Works?

   Step 1. Make a reservation

Input your information (Name, Hotel departure time, Airport Arrival time, Number of Bags, Country, etc) on the application form.

Step 2 : Put your ticket inside the nametag and tie the nametag on your luggage

Tear the ticket apart along the dotted line into 'ticket for customer', and 'ticket for bag'. Keep the 'ticket for customer' and show it to our staff when you find your luggage at the airport, and place the 'ticket for bag' inside the nametag and tie it on your luggage.

If you are staying at 'BAYPAX partner hotel', you may find your nametag at the reception desk, and if you are not staying at one, our staff will bring the nametag to you right at the appointed time.

Step 3 : Leave your bags at the reception desk and Enjoy your day in Seoul!

On the last day of your travel, just leave you luggage at the reception desk at the hotel and enjoy your day! Our BAYPAX staffs will collect your bag at the appointed time, and hand it to you at the airport!

Step 4 : Find your Luggage at the airport

Just turn up at the airport that day or evening, and your luggage will be there waiting for you near by departure gate. Please show your 'ticket for customer' to the BAYPAX staff at the airport, and find your bag. Life made easy by BAYPAX
